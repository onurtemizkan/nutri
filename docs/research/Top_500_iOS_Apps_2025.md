## Executive Summary

The iOS App Store in 2025 is dominated by **AI-powered applications**, with ChatGPT claiming the #1 spot as the most downloaded free iPhone app in the U.S. The gaming sector continues to generate the highest revenue, while social media and entertainment apps maintain massive user bases. The total iOS App Store revenue reached **$138 billion** in 2025.

- **#1 Grossing App:** TikTok ($2.3B revenue)
- **#1 Downloaded Game:** Block Blast!
